Organizations can be added as part of Ignite. They can ask to be added.

Flow: - Organizations contact Ignite. - Ignite creates organization and associates with user. - User can edit organization members and details. - Members can preview the organization.

Organization details: - Name - Slug - Bio - Contact email - Image - Members - Status
